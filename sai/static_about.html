A shifting resource for artists practicing in Nigeria and surfing the web.

  Want to publish an interview about your upcoming project? There are two ways to do this:

1. Click here to download our interview template.* Have a friend interview you using it.** Submit the edited transcipt to us via email.

2. Fill out this form, and when we have availability we will reach out to you to schedule a time for a remote interview.

Our email address is: static@mamasima.site. Write us there for more info :-)


*81KB. You don't need to notify us ahead of time. We publish interviews as we receive them, but according to certain guidelines, which are specified in the interview template.
**You can also interview yourself if you really want. No questions asked.
