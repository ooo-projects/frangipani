Frequently Asked Questions


What's included in a Studio Static e-commerce package?

Studio Static e-commerce packages are front-end solutions. They're custom web-design and development packages for business owners who want unconventional and charming 'storefronts' for their existing online shops. The purchase of an e-commerce package includes the design, development, and publication of a custom storefront displaying your offerings, linked to your payment processor. Best for: service providers, artists, and those who sell digital products.

I sell physical products. Can I purchase an e-commerce package from Studio Static?

The short answer is yes. Studio Static front-end e-commerce packages are suitable for any business-owner who processes orders and payments through an e-commerce platform like Paystack or Selar. However, we strongly recommend that business-owners who sell physical products purchase a support subscription along with their website package. This is so that Studio Static can publish stock, sale, and delivery-related announcements to your website on your behalf.
If you offer physical products that are made-to-order or are fairly consistent in your offerings, then we are a very good solution. On the other hand, our solution may not work for you frequently add or remove products from your catalogue.
After you sign up on the interest form, and before you make your deposit, Studio Static will schedule a discovery call with you to discuss these and other suitability questions.

What if I haven't registered a domain name?

As part of your package, Studio Static will offer guidance on registering a domain name. We will also set up hosting for your website for you using GitHub Pages.

What if I already have a website? Can I still purchase an e-commerce package?

If you already have a website and would like to add a Studio Static website shop to it, then we may be able to help you. Contact us at support@mamasima.site with the specifics of your enquiry, and we will let you know.



Still have questions?

We encourage you to register your interest so that we can schedule a 20-minute discovery call with you. If you're not quite ready for a phone call, you can reach us by email at support@mamasima.site.
