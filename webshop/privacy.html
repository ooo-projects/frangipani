Privacy Policy


General Information

“Personal data” is any data that allows someone to identify you. This website (https://www.mamasima.site) does not collect, process or store your personal data. However, if you choose to work with Mamasima Studio or to sign up for our newsletter, we may collect some personal data, such as your contact information, to use as a means of communication with you and to improve our services.

Similarly, if you purchase any of our products or services using Paystack, then we may collect some of your personal data, such as your contact and payment information, through Paystack in order to process your transaction. These data are not stored by Mamasima Studio.

Under no circumstances is your data ever sold or rented by Mamasima Studio to a third party.

The ways in which Mamasima Studio collects and processes your data are outlined in the policy below.

Your Consent

By using this website, you are consenting to the use of your information as set forth in this Policy. If you do not agree to be bound by this Policy, you may not access or use this website.

You may withdraw your consent later by ceasing to use this website, unsubscribing from our newsletter, and emailing us at support@mamasima.site to request that we remove your data.

Clients & Customers

If you engaged in 1:1 services with Mamasima Studio, then Mamasima Studio collected your contact information (name and email address) through an interest form. In the course of our work, we may also have collected your phone number.

This data is held within Paystack. Studio Static is the web design and development arm of Mamasima Studio. Mamasima Studio does not store or process your payment information internally. The privacy policy of third-party companies such as Paystack applies to their collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

The purpose of this information:
Your name, email address, and phone number enable us to communicate with you about our products or services. Your payment information enables us to process your transactions with us through Paystack.

How long information is kept:
Personal data that you give to third-party companies such as Paystack is subject to the privacy policy of those companies. Mamasima Studio retains your personal data until you unsubscribe or notify us otherwise.
