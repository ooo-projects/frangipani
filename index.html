⊸ Three of Wands ⊸
1. Virtue is synonymous with capacity. Do you feel morally under-resourced? Look within. What tools do you already have at your disposal, in terms of care and courtesy? Use these to help you take your next step. 2. What's good for the goose is good for the gander, where the goose is your wellbeing and the gander is your ego. If an aligned action brings you closer to your goal, then it has served its purpose perfectly, and it would be rude to despise it for not having taken you all the way. 3. Taking one step at a time is not the same as cutting corners. Be pragmatic to reduce stress and sustain your energy.
