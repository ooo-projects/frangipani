Mamasima Magazine Issue #1

Mamasima Magazine is a hand-bound literary periodical. Issue one was circulated at cultural events in Lagos, Nigeria in November and December of 2025. The magazine was accompanied with a number of handcrafted objects (hand-beaded adire bookmarks made from offcut fabric, oil pastels, hand-bound memo notebooks, lunar calendars, and more, by Oritsemughone Ogbemi). You are now browsing the digital version.
