What is this service?
Studio Static is a web design and development practice. Through it, artist Oritsemughone Ogbemi creates elegant, responsive websites for other artists and their organizations using plain html & css.

How does it work?
Interested artists fill out a form. After that, the process of building your website occurs in four stages:
  • Stage 1: A 45-minute call to talk through your ideas
  • Stage 2: A drafting period, during which I create a sample site for you to review. This will usually involve two rounds of feedback and take ~three weeks.
  • Stage 3: Publication of your approved website. The site is also connected to a domain name, if you have one.
  • Stage 4: A final training call (45 mins), during which I show you how to update your website when you need to.
 Who is this for?
This service is best suited to artists and orgs that want to display their information (such as bylines, lists of services, portfolios, biographies, links & contact details) on one-to-four page websites that don't require much maintenance.
